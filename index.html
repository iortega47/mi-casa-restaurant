Welcome To Mi Casa Restaurant

About Us

The history of Mexican food is a long and diverse one. It is believed that authentic Mexican food might have been derived from the Mayan Indians. For nearly thirty years, Mi Casa Restaurant has taken pride in bringing this refined cuisine from our kitchen to your table.

Our Specialty Cuisine

Enchiladas

Enchiladas are corn tortillas rolled around a filling and covered with a savory sauce.

Tacos

One handmade puffy tortilla with with choice of filling topped with lettuce and tomatoes.

Tamales

Tamales are made from a mixture of corn dough (masa) and filling, wrapped in a corn husk and then steamed.

Tortas

A Mexican sandwich served on toasted bread. Includes meat of choice, lettuce, tomato, sour cream, cheese and beans.

Chicken Fajitas

Chicken is seasoned, seared and cooked to perfection, then tossed with sauteed bell peppers and onions.

Pozole

Traditional Mexican pozole is a rich, brothy soup made with pork, hominy, and red chiles.

Sopes

Three handmade thick tortillas topped with refried beans, choice of meat. Topped with lettuce, pico de gallo and sour cream

Chilles Rellenos

Poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, then coated in a fluffy egg batter and fried until golden brown.

Churros

Churros are long cake-like pastry covered with sugar & cinnamon filled with your choice of strawberry, vanilla or chocolate.

Conchas

The conchas consist of a soft and tender sweet bread topped with a crunchy coating that resembles the grooves of a seashell.

Flan

Delicious and mouth watering custard made from egg yolk and milk, topped with caramel syrup.

Tres Leches

Tres Leches Cake is a moist sponge cake soaked in three different types of milk.

Aguas Frescas

Agua frescas are refreshing fruit drinks made simply of fruit blended with water.

Margaritas

Margarita is a tangy mixture of rich grassy, tequila, tart fresh lime, sweet, citrusy orange liqueur and a savoury salt rim.
